2019 NJ Assembly Elections


Every New Jersey legislative district is represented in the State Assembly by 2 Representatives and in the State Senate by 1 Senator.
In 2019, NJ citizens will vote for the State Representatives. On June 4th 2019 there will be primary elections (candidates filing deadline is 4/1/2019) and on November 5th 2019 there will be General Election (filing deadline is 6/4/2019).
Some of the most important topics under discussion in the State Congress are:
  • NJ State Budget and Fiscal Crisis
  • Affordable Housing
  • Mass Transit (NJTransit, Gateway Tunnel)
  • Environment (Proceed with fossil fuels project or move towards clean energy)
  • Redistricting (The new State Congress will oversee the 2010 Census and Redistricting)
  • Immigration (Driver License, cooperation with ICE)
  • Education
  • Healthcare (expand Medicaid and other State programs)
  • NJ Government transparency and efficiency
